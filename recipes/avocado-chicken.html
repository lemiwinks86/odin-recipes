Avocado Chicken Salad

A healthy, quick lunch salad


Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, poached and cut into small pieces
  • 2 avocados, cubed
  • 1 small mango, cubed
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes, quartered
  • 1/2 cup fresh or frozen corn
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced

For Dressing

  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon minced jalapeno
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste

How to make

  1. Make the dressing
    • In a medium bowl, mix all dressing ingredients and whisk to combine them
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste
  2. In a large bowl, combine salad ingredients and the dressing
  3. Toss the salad until it is evenly coated in dressing
  4. If needed, season again to taste with salt and pepper
