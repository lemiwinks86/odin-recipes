Cheese and Mushroom Omelette

A golden brown, folded omelette filled with cheese and veggies


Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cheddar cheese

How to make

  1. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a skillet over medium-high
  2. Add mushrooms and saute until golden brown (about 3 minutes) then place mushrooms in a bowl
  3. Break eggs into a bowl and mix/beat until completely blended and frothy
  4. Stir salt and pepper into egg mix
  5. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in the same skillet over medium-high
  6. Add eggs and stir them around (use rubber heatproof spatula), pulling from the sides to the middle
  7. After about 2 minutes, spread the mushroom mixture over half of the eggs and sprinkle the cheese on top
  8. When the eggs are brown on the bottom, fold them over the filling and serve.
